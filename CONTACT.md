# Contact Information

For inquiries about our Solana trading tools and services, please reach out to us:

## Primary Contact Methods

1. **GitHub Issues**
   - Visit our repository: https://github.com/hs365/solana-trading-tools
   - Open a new issue with your inquiry
   - We monitor issues regularly and respond promptly

2. **Telegram**
   - You can contact us through this bot's associated Telegram channel
   - Share your project requirements and we'll get back to you

## What to Include in Your Inquiry

When reaching out, please provide:

- Description of your requirements
- Timeline expectations
- Budget considerations
- Any specific features you need

## Response Time

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. For urgent requests, please mention it in your message.

## Support

Once engaged in a project, we provide full support during development and implementation.
